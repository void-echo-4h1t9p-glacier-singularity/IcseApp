About Swaraj India Public School

Excellence in Education Since 1995

Our Legacy

Swaraj India Public School, Kanpur, established in 1995, is a premier ICSE affiliated institution committed to providing quality education. Located in the heart of Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, we have been nurturing young minds for over 29 years.

Our school is affiliated with the Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE) and follows the ICSE curriculum, ensuring comprehensive education that prepares students for global challenges.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a student-centric approach, SIPS has consistently produced outstanding results in ICSE examinations.
